How to buy crypto currencies like XRP, TRX, IOTA, XVG which are not available in coinbase ?

To buy crypto currencies not available in coinbase, you will have to buy them through an exchange.
Most exchanges operate only in crypto currencies and do not accept credit/debit cards.
The way prescribed below involves buying ethereum in coinbase, transferring it to an exchange and then using the ethereum to buy other cryptocurrencies.

  • Create an account in Coinbase. https://www.coinbase.com
  • After creating an account, add a payment method. For quick setup, use credit/debit card. For larger amounts, set up debit from your bank account. This setup may take time, at times more than a week.
  • Once payment method is set, click on Buy/Sell and select Ethereum
  • Select your payment method
  • Enter the amount in USD to buy
  • Click on "Buy Ethereum Instantly" and confirm.
  • You should see your crypto currency balance under "Accounts" and "Dashboard"
  • Register on Crypto Currency Exchange : Binance https://www.binance.com/?ref=12268205
  • Once registration is completed, login and click on "Funds -> Deposits and withdrawls"
  • Type "ETH" in the search box (ETH : Ethereum)
  • Click on "Deposit"
  • Copy the ETH Deposit Address. This is the Ethereum wallet address, where you will be transferring funds from Coinbase
  • Go back to Coinbase. Click on Accounts -> Ethereum Wallet
  • Click on send and enter the ETH Deposit Address. Enter the amount to be sent. Note the Network Fee. It varies depending on network congestion and load.
  • Click continue and confirm
  • Go back to Binance. Click on Funds -> History. You should see your transaction in processing state. It usually takes 10-15 mins but may take more, depending on network load.
  • Once completed, click on Funds -> Deposits Withdrawls
  • Click on "Hide small assets" checkbox. You should see your Ethereum Balance here.
  • Time to trade : Click on Exchange (Basic)
  • On the right side, click on ETH (Represents Ethereum Markets : Use ethereum to buy other crypto currencies)
  • As an example, to buy Ripple (XRP), type XRP in the search box. Click on XRP/ETH
  • XRP performance chart is loaded.
  • You can buy XRP using Limit/Market/Stop-Limit type of trades.
  • To buy limit, enter the amount of XRP coins to buy. (Binance fee is 0.050%)
  • Click buy XRP. If successful, it should show up in Funds -> Deposits Withdrawls

Congratulations, you are now an owner of a crypto currency you believe in!
