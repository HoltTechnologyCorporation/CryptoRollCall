About us

With the rise of crypto currency, the number of currency exchanges has risen exponentially. Crypto assets are distributed across a number of exchanges and wallets. Getting a quick overview of the entire portfolio is cumbersome.

CryptoRollCall

  • Provides a snapshot view of the entire crypto portfolio.
  • Upcoming features include automatic sync with exchanges, signup to support multiple users and portfolios, crypto performance charts and personalized news and announcements regarding currencies you own.
  • Calculates net worth of the investments, using real time data, in numerous global currencies
  • Shows price fluctuations across an hour, a day and a week
  • Provides utilities to convert from/to crypto currency and FIAT
