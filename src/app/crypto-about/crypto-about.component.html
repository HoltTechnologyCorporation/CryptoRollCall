About us

With the rise of crypto currency, the number of currency exchanges has risen exponentially. It would be convenient to get a snapshot view of the investment portfolio, without logging in to various exchanges, and calculating using excel sheets or calculators.

CryptoRollCall

  • Snapshot view of the entire crypto portfolio.
  • Net worth of the investments, using real time data, in numerous global currencies
  • Helpful visualizations for tracking assets
  • Track price fluctuations across an hour, a day and a week
  • Utilities to convert from/to crypto currency and FIAT
  • Upcoming features include automatic sync with exchanges, signup to support multiple users and portfolios, crypto performance charts and personalized news and announcements regarding currencies you own.
