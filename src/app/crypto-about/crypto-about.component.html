About us

Problem Statement :

With the rise of crypto currency, the number of currency exchanges has risen exponentially. Crypto assets are distributed across a number of exchanges and wallets. Getting a quick overview of the entire portfolio is cumbersome.

Solution :

CryptoRollCall aims to solve that problem by :-
-  Providing a snapshot view of the entire crypto portfolio
-  Calculating net worth of the investments, using real time data
-  Showing price fluctuations across an hour, a day and a week
-  Providing utilities to convert from/to crypto currency and USD

How to :

To get started
-  Click on the next to the crypto currency you own.
-  Click on Portfolio
-  Update the amount of crypto currencies you own
-  Total net worth calculated real time on latest prices
-  Bookmark the page for quick navigation
